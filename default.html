Get Urls for Chrome

Chrome Extension to get URLs of open tabs, and display them in a single list in a popup window, allowing one to easily copy and paste urls of all open tabs for future reference.
Additionally:
  • Clicking the url in the popup window highlights the tab.
  • Clicking "close all tabs but current" link in upper right hand corner closes all but the current, highlighted tab.

Known Issues:
  • Unexpected results when more than one Chrome window is open
  • List does not clear completely when "close all is clicked."
